# Job Portal - User Guide

Welcome to the Job Portal! This guide explains how to navigate and use the platform from two distinct perspectives: the **Jobseeker (Applier)** and the **Recruiter**.

---

## 👨‍💼 Guide for Recruiters

As a recruiter, your primary goal is to represent your company, post job openings, and find the best talent. 

### 1. Account Registration & Login
*   Navigate to the **Login / Sign Up** page.
*   Register a new account and explicitly select your role as **Recruiter**.
*   *Alternatively*, you can use **Google Sign-In** for quick access (ensure your profile is set to the Recruiter role).

### 2. Setting Up Your Company Profile
Before you can post any jobs, you must register the company you are hiring for.
*   Navigate to the **Companies** section from your dashboard navigation.
*   Click **"Register New Company"**.
*   Fill in the required details: Company Name, Description, Website URL, and upload a professional **Company Logo**.
*   *Note: A single recruiter account can create and manage multiple companies.*

### 3. Posting a New Job
Once your company profile is active, you can start posting jobs.
*   Go to the **Jobs** section in your Recruiter Dashboard and click **"Post a Job"** (or similar "New Job" button).
*   Provide comprehensive details: Job Title, Detailed Description, Required Skills, Salary Range, Location, Job Type (Full-time, Part-time, etc.), and Experience Level.
*   Select the associated Company for this job posting.
*   Submit the form. The job is now live and visible to all jobseekers.

### 4. Reviewing & Managing Applicants
*   Go to your **Dashboard** to view all the jobs you have posted.
*   Click on a specific job posting to see the list of candidates who have applied.
*   **⭐ Priority Candidates:** Notice that candidates with an active **Premium Subscription** will automatically appear at the top of your applicant list.
*   Click on an applicant to view their full profile, which includes their uploaded **Resume (PDF)**, contact details, bio, and listed skills.

## 👩‍💻 Guide for Jobseekers (Appliers)

As a jobseeker, your goal is to build a strong profile, find relevant job listings, and apply successfully.

### 1. Account Registration & Login
*   Navigate to the **Login / Sign Up** page.
*   Create an account and ensure you select the **Jobseeker** role.
*   You can also use **Google Sign-In** for a faster registration process.

### 2. Completing Your Profile (Mandatory for Applying)
Before you can apply to any job, you *must* have a complete profile.
*   Navigate to your **Profile** page.
*   **Upload Resume:** You cannot apply for jobs without a resume. Upload your latest CV in **PDF format**.
*   **Add Skills:** Tag your profile with relevant technical or soft skills (e.g., React, Node.js, Marketing).
*   **Update Bio & Picture:** Add a professional summary and upload a profile picture to stand out.

### 3. Searching for Jobs
*   Navigate to the **Jobs** or **Browse Jobs** page.
*   Use the search bar and filter sidebar to narrow down jobs by Keywords, Location, or Salary.
*   Click on any job card to view the full description, company details, and requirements.

### 4. Applying for Jobs
*   On the Job Details page, click the **"Apply Now"** button.
*   The system will automatically attach your uploaded resume to the application.
*   *Note:* You cannot apply to the same job twice.
*   **Email Notification:** Upon successful application, you will receive an automated confirmation email detailing the job and company you applied to.

### 5. Tracking Your Applications
*   Go to the **My Applications** section within your profile or dashboard.
*   Here, you can see a history of all the jobs you have applied for, including the job title, company name, and the status of your application.

### 6. Upgrading to Premium (Optional)
If you want to maximize your chances of being seen by recruiters:
*   Navigate to the **Subscription / Pricing** page.
*   Purchase a premium plan via the secure Razorpay checkout.
*   **The Advantage:** As long as your subscription is active, your applications will be visually highlighted and moved to the **very top** of the recruiter's applicant list, giving you priority visibility over non-subscribed users.

## 💡 Technical Notes / Troubleshooting
*   **Email Deliverability:** If you don't see your application confirmation email, check your Spam or Promotions folder.
*   **File Uploads:** Profile pictures and resumes are securely stored on Cloudinary. Ensure your files are within the acceptable size limits (usually under 5MB).
*   **Payments:** Subscription payments are processed safely through Razorpay. Your subscription status updates instantly upon successful payment.
